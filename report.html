About Sai Krishna Residency

Sai Krishna Residency is a residential apartment completed by Radiance Infraventure. It is situated in Chandrakiran Nagar in Nagpur. It consists of 2 and 3BHK apartments with all modern amenities. The respective floor areas of 2 and 3BHK spaces are 900 sq. ft. and 1,115 sq. ft. each. It is a recent launch and is currently under-construction. It is scheduled to be in possession by March 2022. The total area of the space is 1.11 acre. The locality is surrounded by essential facilities like schools, hospitals, banks, parks, and malls. Besides, several important roads are passing through the region that ensures good connectivity to the surroundings. It shares a boundary with Ayodhya Nagar, Manewada, Dighori, and other important localities. Overall, the neighborhood suits the residential purposes Chandrakiran Nagar is located 9.9 from NagpurJunction Railway Station and 10.5 km from Dr.Babasaheb International Airport One of the most attractive upcoming apartments in the region, it offers a great option for interested buyers.
